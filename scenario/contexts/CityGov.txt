**Fee Schedule (§ = Novaterra Dollar)**
DOCUMENTS:
- Driver's License: §45 (8-year), §30 (4-year)
- Passport (City processing): §165 standard, §195 with card
- Birth Certificate: §25 per certified copy
- Marriage License: §85
- Death Certificate: §25

PERMITS:
- Building Permit: 1.5% of project value (min §75)
- Business License: §200/year general, §75 home-based
- Special Event: §50-500 based on size/type
- Residential Parking: §50/year per vehicle

RECREATION:
- Pool Daily Pass: §5 adult, §3 child/senior
- Recreation Center: §40/month, §400/year
- Dog Park Permit: §25/year
- Facility Rental: §50-500/hour

**Transit Information**
- Single Bus Fare: §2.50
- Day Pass: §7.00
- Monthly Pass: §85
- Senior/Student/Disabled: 50% discount
- Light Rail Zones: Same as bus fare
- Park & Ride: §3/day
- Real-time Info: MeridianTransit app or text STOP+[number] to 77890

**Waste Collection Zones**
- Zone A (North): Trash Mon/Thu, Recycling Tue, Organics Fri
- Zone B (Central): Trash Tue/Fri, Recycling Wed, Organics Mon
- Zone C (South/East): Trash Wed/Sat, Recycling Thu, Organics Tue
- Bulk Pickup: First Saturday monthly (48-hour advance booking)
- Holiday Schedule: No service on city holidays, pickup delayed one day

**Property Tax Information**
- Due Dates: April 30, October 31
- Senior Exemption: Age 65+, income <§45,000, 50% reduction
- Payment Options: Online (free e-check, 2.3% credit card fee), mail, in-person, auto-pay
- Late Penalty: 1.5% monthly
- Payment Plans: Available for §2,000+

**Voter Registration Requirements**
- Age: 18+ by election day
- Residency: 30 days in precinct
- ID Requirements: Valid photo ID or two forms of identification
- Registration Methods: Online, by mail, in-person
- Deadline: 30 days before election (same-day with proof of residency)
- Polling Hours: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM election day

**Public Benefits Programs**
- Senior Services (65+): Transit discount, meal programs, recreation access
- Low-Income Utility Assistance: <§35,000 household income
- Youth Programs: Free/reduced summer camps, after-school care
- Food Assistance: Applications at Social Services or online
- Emergency Housing: Coordinated through 311

**Development & Zoning**
- R1: Single-family residential (min 6,000 sq ft)
- R2: Multi-family residential (max 3 stories)
- C1: Commercial (retail/office)
- M1: Mixed use (residential over commercial)
- Industrial zones: M2 (light), M3 (heavy)
- Zoning changes: 14-day public notice required
- Interactive map: portal.meridiansprings.gov/zoning
